BIOLAB consists of several units
integrated in the rack structure
and a mobile Experiment
Container ( EC ).

The EC is the interface of the
experiments to BIOLAB´s various
units.
Fig: ec_animation
The incubator is a closed controlled environment
that supports the life of biological specimen inside
their ECs.

The Incubator in particular provides
  • a thermal environment which can be regulated
    between 18 and 40°Celsius,
  • an atmospheric environment where gas composition,
    pressure and humidity are maintained within predefined limits
  • a gravitational environment between µg and 2g.
  • illumination/observation of specimen

Fig: Incubator
Two identical Centrifuges accomodate the
ECs during incubation.

The centrifuges provide a microgravity environment
when held steady or reference accelerations from 10-3 to 2g.
