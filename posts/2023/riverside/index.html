Riverside

15 October, 2023

A nice rock concert at Rockhal on Sunday evening, watching Polish band Riverside, supported by Dutch group Lesoir.

Lesoir

The support act, Lesoir, were good, with some nice songs. I felt they struggled a little to connect with the audience.

Riverside

Riverside were fantastic and put on a great show for the audience. The lead vocalist had a dry sense of humour which added to the atmosphere. No they are not like Dream Theatre, or Anathema. 64 members of the audience were from Luxembourg, the rest from elsewhere.
