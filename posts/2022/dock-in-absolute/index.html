Dock in Absolute

26 August, 2022

Weird name, lovely music.

This jazz trio was playing at the opening of the Moselmusikfestival at the Kulturhafen Zurlauben in Trier. The artists are Jean-Philippe Koch, Arne Wiegand and Victor Kraus. Only the first is listed as band member on the website. I guess that's how it is with jazz bands. All three seem to be connected to the Conservatoire in Luxembourg.
