Chance

24 December, 2022

I was going through a quiz today in which I realised, after 3 of 50 questions, I had no idea about any of the answers. I thought I'd keep going, on average I should get between 12 and 13 correct. As I was going through it, I thought I got almost all of them wrong. Turns out that "almost" was about one in four. I got exactly the amount correct that a monkey would.

Glad to see that the laws of averages are still alive and kicking in 2022.

Merry Christmas!
